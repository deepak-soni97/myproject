Description

This lesson examines key 19th century American events - the 1820 Compromise, the Mexican-American War, the Mexican Cession, and the Oregon Compromise. The lesson challenges students to evaluate the significance of each event with relation to expansion of American slavery, the drive towards civil war, and the growth of America as a continental power.
